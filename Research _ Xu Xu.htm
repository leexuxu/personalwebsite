Research

I am a comparative politics scholar who studies authoritarianism and Chinese Politics. My current research agenda examines digital surveillance in China. A secondary research agenda of mine focuses on corruption and development. My work combines an attention to causal inference using experiments and archival data with game theory and extensive fieldwork in China.
